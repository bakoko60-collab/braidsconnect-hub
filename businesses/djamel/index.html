African & Caribbean Products

GROCERIES · BEAUTY · AFRICAN FASHION

Djamel African Market brings together the flavors and everyday products of Africa and the Caribbean under one roof. From pantry staples to special treats, we focus on the brands you grew up with and the new favorites you’ll discover along the way.

Browse yams, plantains, rice, spices, flours, canned goods, oils, beverages, and more—plus a strong selection of beauty and personal care products trusted in homes and salons across the diaspora.
